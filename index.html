    Since 2019

    Brazil

    Since 2019, when Paulo co-advised Christian's final graduation theses, the founders have been working together. While working at AeroTechLab, they developed research on human-robot physical interaction.

    They are currently working together on another project supported by EMBRAPII which is focused on the development of an agricultural robot. Christian is computer vision coordinator. He leads a team responsible for detecting and classifying soybean and cotton leaves using deep learning. The objective is to make the robot capable of differentiating both species making the robot able to remove cotton plants in soybean crops, and soybean plants in cotton crops. Paulo is the general manager of the project. He is responsible for leading the entire team making the interfaces between hardware and software.

    After some time working on the EMBRAPII project, Paulo and Christian identified that although Brazilian agricultors have tech connectivity in their farms, the data manipulation and interpretation are still in need of solutions. This was the context where Nimbus Smart Vision (NSV) - a company focused on data - was founded by Christian and Paulo.

    NSV is supported on 3 main concepts: cloud computing, machine learning and computer vision. Modularity and generalization are the main concepts of the NSV work methodology. All features created are thought to be unrestricted and able to work in another project case. Due to this fact, NSV is very scalable and has a bright future to grow helping more people to solve their problems.
